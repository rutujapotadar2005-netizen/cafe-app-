Our Story

Born from a restless love for coffee.

Red Eye Café was born in 2019 from a single obsession: the perfect cup. Founded by two coffee-fanatic friends who couldn't sleep — not from insomnia, but from excitement about the world of specialty coffee — we set out to create a space unlike any other.

We named it after the "red eye" — a shot of espresso in drip coffee, for those who need to push through. It became our philosophy: bold, energetic, unapologetic. We source only from farms we've visited, roast only what we'd drink ourselves, and serve only what we're proud of.

Today, Red Eye is more than a café. It's a community of early risers, late-night creators, and everyone in between who believes that a great cup of coffee can change your day.
